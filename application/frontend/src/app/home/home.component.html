Home Page

Welcome to the home page of the Cliq Payment Transactions app!

In the "Incoming Transactions" page, you can see all saved transactions where you are the creditor.

Similarly, the "Outgoing Transactions" page will show you all transactions where you are the debtor.

In the "Add Transaction" page, you can fill out a form to add a new transaction.

You will need to log in to access these pages. If you are not logged in, you will be redirected to the login page.
